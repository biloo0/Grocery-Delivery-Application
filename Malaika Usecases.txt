Create Order :
 User will login/Signup into the application.
 Provide location access and phone number.
 Browse categories of grocery items .
 Browse items, select items and their quantities (if in
bulk user will be notified to provide urgency details).
 Add desired items to cart.
 Provide payment details and select payment options.
 Place Order

Delivery:
 Order Confirmation
 Address Confirmation
 Assign orders to the delivery boy area wise.
 Deliver order to their destinations
